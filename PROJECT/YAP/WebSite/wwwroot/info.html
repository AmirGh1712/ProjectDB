THE PROJECT

This is a project for our database workshop course.
In here, you can search for attractions near your location in a specified radius of your choice.
You can keep an account in our database, add reviews from your account, look at other reviews etc.
You can also give parameters to our search engine, if you are looking for something specific such as a restaurant.
We hope you enjoy YAP.
